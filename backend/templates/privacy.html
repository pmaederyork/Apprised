Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Apprised, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our multi-agent AI conversation and document collaboration platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for account identification and authentication
  • Name - Displayed in your profile
  • Profile picture - Displayed in your account (optional)

1.2 Google Drive Access

When you grant Google Drive permissions, we can access files that you explicitly choose to share with our application. This includes:

  • Document content - Files you open or save through Apprised
  • File metadata - Names, modification dates, and file structure

Important: We only access files that you explicitly choose to use with Apprised. We do not scan, index, or access other files in your Google Drive.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect minimal usage information:

  • Authentication tokens - Stored securely to maintain your session
  • Error logs - Used to improve app stability (no personal data included)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication - Verify your identity and maintain secure access
  • Document Collaboration - Enable rich text editing and AI-assisted document features
  • Google Drive Integration - Save and retrieve documents you choose to work with
  • Service Improvement - Understand how to make Apprised better (anonymized data only)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Client-Side Storage

All your conversations, documents, and settings are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device
  • We do not store your conversation content on our servers
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your local Apprised data

3.2 Server-Side Data

Our servers only temporarily handle:

  • Authentication tokens - Stored in encrypted cookies for session management
  • API requests - Processed in real-time and not stored

We do not store your:

  • Conversation history
  • Document content
  • AI prompts or responses
  • Files or attachments

3.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with Google
  • JWT tokens for session management
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and Google Drive API for optional document storage. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Terms of Service

4.2 Anthropic API

AI conversations are processed through Anthropic's Claude API. Your API key (if provided) is stored locally in your browser. Messages sent to Claude are subject to Anthropic's privacy policy:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Local Data

Since data is stored locally in your browser:

  • You can delete all data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Data persists only on your device
  • We cannot recover deleted local data

5.2 Account Data

To delete your account and associated authentication data:

  • Sign out of Apprised
  • Revoke Apprised's access in your Google Account permissions
  • Contact us at the email below if you need assistance

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - Review data stored in your browser's localStorage
  • Delete your data - Clear browser storage or revoke app access
  • Revoke permissions - Remove Google Drive access at any time
  • Export your data - Use browser developer tools to export localStorage
  • Opt-out of Google services - Use Apprised without Google Drive integration

7. Children's Privacy

Apprised is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Apprised after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Users

Apprised is operated from the United States. If you are accessing Apprised from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

10. No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use:

  • Third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising networks
  • Marketing automation

Your privacy is our priority, and we believe in minimal data collection.
